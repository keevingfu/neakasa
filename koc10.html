Keywords Analysis Insights

Key Findings

  • Functional keywords (self-cleaning, smart control) have the highest search volume, indicating users' focus on product practicality
  • Health monitoring-related long-tail keywords are growing rapidly, reflecting an increase in users' concern about pet health
  • Keywords related to multi-cat and large-cat scenarios show stable search volume, indicating sustained demand from specific user groups
  • Design-related keywords have smaller search volumes but higher conversion rates, indicating users' willingness to pay for aesthetic designs

Strategic Recommendations

  • Optimize SEO layout for functional keywords to improve organic search rankings
  • Develop targeted marketing content for multi-cat households, enhancing scene-based promotion
  • Increase content marketing related to health monitoring features to meet users' concerns about pet health
  • Invest in targeted ads for design-related keywords to improve conversion of high-value users
